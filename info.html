Our Projects

VEC

VIT-AP Clubs & Events


An institutional-based App which is very useful for the students and faculties of this institution.

Events

This app provides all the upcoming event’s data with their venue, time and date details clearly. We provide information regarding the University Events, Club Events, Guest Lectures.

Clubs

It provides all the data of all clubs profile wise. Clubs can post their posters of announcements regarding club recruitments, Events, Event recruitments, etc. They can provide club recruitment forms and can advertise about their activities.

Users

Users can scroll through the posters and get to know about the information. Direct messaging to the Clubs is available. Users can get to know about the club details and respective information regarding a particular club.


And also, All the important Notifications from college will be provided in this app. This has many features included in it. The student, the Club , the Administration are the 3 interferences provided within this app. Only the respective one can get access to the respective department he/she is related to.
